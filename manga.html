This is just a brief list of some of my favorite mangas. I really like mangas with themes of romance and psychological. However the romance theme has to not be the main point of the plot, such as it being the main point of a manga like horimiya, Ao Haru Ride, Kimi ni Todoke, or other bland vanilla romcom trash shoujo manga. My favorite type of romance is when two people are actually meant for each other, for example the type of romance in "The flowers of Evil" is a perfect example of this.

My absolute favorit manga would be "The Flowers of Evil" and "Goodnight Punpun". These combine my absolute favorite themes in manga, those being, romance and psychological.
